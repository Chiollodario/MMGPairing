Access the device internal storage:
adb shell

Access the proper smartphone folder:
cd /storage/emulated/0/shared/Drawing_Data

Access the proper smartwatch folder:
cd /storage/emulated/0/shared/Accelerometer_Data

Transfer data from the device (exit from adb, going to a normal PowerShell):
adb pull /storage/emulated/0/shared/ C:\Users\DARIO-DELL\Desktop\
